Wiki 1 — Me pēhea te mahi o te AI

Ngā whāinga ako

  • Whakamāramatia, mā ngā kupu māmā, te mahi a te large language model (LLM) me te mihini whakaputa whakaahua.
  • Whakawehea te AI whakapae, te AI whakarōpū, me te AI whakaputa.
  • Mōhiotia kei hea kē te AI i roto i ngā taputapu o ia rā (rapunga, hononga pāpori, taputapu whakaahua, autocorrect).

Ngā ariā matua

He pūtahi tauira-whakataurite ngā pūnaha AI, kua whakangungua ki ngā pātaka raraunga rahi. Kāore rātou i te “mōhio” pērā i te tangata — ka whakaputa rātou i ngā putanga e tūpono ana, e ai ki ngā mea kua kitea i mua. Ko tēnei whakaaro kotahi te take o te nuinga o ngā mea ka tae mai ā muri: ko te take ka pohehe te AI, ko te take ka whakaata i te whakatoihara, ko te take ka maia tonu te reo o te AI ahakoa e hē ana.

Ngā pātai kōrerorero

  1. I mua i tēnei wiki, he aha tāu i whakaaro ai mō te mahi a te AI ina pātai koe? E pēhea te tata o tāu whakaaro ki te pono?
  2. Kei hea koe e kite ana i te AI i roto i ngā taputapu kē e whakamahia ana e koe? Hangaia he rārangi tahi.
  3. Ki te whakapae noa te AI i te kupu e whai mai ana, he aha ngā momo pātai e pai ana, he aha ngā momo pātai e kino ana?
  4. Taiohi ki te mātua: he aha tētahi mea i hiahia koe kia mōhio te pakeke mō te whakamahi a tōu whakatipuranga i te AI? Mātua ki te taiohi: he aha tētahi mea i hiahia koe kia mārama ake koe?

Mahi-ā-kāinga: “Kotahi te pātai, e toru ngā taputapu”

Whiriwhiria he pātai tūwhera e aroha ana kōrua (hei tauira: “He aha tā tāua tunu mō tēnei wiki, $60 noa te utu?” “He aha ngā take o WWI?”). Pātaihia te pātai kotahi i ngā taputapu e toru — pērā i a ChatGPT, Google Gemini, me te rapunga Google māori. Whakatauritehia ngā whakautu: - Kei hea rātou e whakaae ana? - Kei hea rātou e tautohe ana? - Ko tēhea i tāpiri i ngā puna? He puna pono? - Ko tēhea i maia rawa atu? He ōrite te maia ki te tika?

Tuhia kotahi rerenga kōrero ki ia tangata mō ngā mea i miharo ai.
