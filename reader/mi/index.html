Tirohanga whānui o te Akoranga

He akoranga tēnei mō ngā mātua me ngā taiohi (mai i te 12 tau ki te 18 tau pea) hei mahi tahi. He kaupapa ano tēnā wiki, me ngā pānuitanga tahi, he kōrerorero ārahi, me he mahi-ā-ringa kia whai wāhi tahi ai te whānau ki te whakamātau i te AI — kaua ko ia tangata ki tana rūma anake.

Te āhua: E ono ngā wāhanga ā-wiki, 60–90 meneti te roa o ia wāhanga Te hunga whāia: Ngā tokorua mātua-taiohi (rōpū whānau iti rānei) Te whāinga: Hei te mutunga o te akoranga, ka whai te whānau i te kupu mahi tahi mō te AI, he whakaaetanga ā-whānau mō tōna whakamahinga, me ngā hangarau whakaaro arohaehae kia ngā tonu te ako i te hurihanga o te hangarau.

Te hanga o ia wāhanga: 1. Ngā whāinga ako — ko ngā mea ka taea e koe i te mutunga 2. Ngā ariā matua — he pānui poto, he ataata rānei hei mahi takitahi i mua i te hui 3. Ngā pātai kōrerorero — ngā pātai hei mahi tahi 4. Te mahi-ā-kāinga — he mahi tūturu hei mahi tokorua 5. He rauemi mā ngā mātua — he tuhinga taunaki pono 6. Whakaaroaro — he pātai mō te rārangi mahara whakamutunga
