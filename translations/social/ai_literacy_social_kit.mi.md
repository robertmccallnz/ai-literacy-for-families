## Panui 2 — He take ā-papatipu te muna

**Taupatupatu:** Ko te muna he hua utu, ehara i te mōtika.

### Twitter/X
Kua hokona te muna hei āhuatanga utu nui.

Ko te tikanga, ka aru tonutia ngā whānau mahi, ka taea e ngā whānau whai rawa te wete atu.

I te Wiki 3, ka akona ngā taiohi ki te kite i te tohu utu kei runga i ia taputapu "utukore".

robertmccallnz.github.io/ai-literacy-for-families

### Pukamata
Kua kite koe e piki tonu ana te utu o te muna?

Ka mātakitaki te whakaputanga "utukore" i a koe. Ka iti ake te mātakitaki o te whakaputanga utu. Ka oati te whakaputanga teitei ake kāore e mātakitaki. Kua huri te muna hei take papatipu, kei roto ā tātou tamariki e tipu ake ana.

Ka hoatu e te Wiki 3 o tēnei akoranga Mātauranga AI mō ngā Whānau ngā taputapu kia mārama ai, kia whakatau tahi ai te whānau ko ēhea e pai ana ki te whakawhiti, ko ēhea kāore.

Pānuihia i te ipurangi, tāngia te pukapuka mahi, tohaina rānei ki tō kura: https://robertmccallnz.github.io/ai-literacy-for-families/

### LinkedIn
Kua āta hurihia te muna hei taumata utu. Ko te wheako taunoa ko te mātakitaki; ko te tiaki raraunga whaitake, kua whakapikia hei āhuatanga utu nui.

He hua kino tēnei mō ngā whānau e iti ake ana ngā pūtea, ka kawea e rātou ngā utu raraunga o ngā hua "utukore" e taea ai e ngā kaiwhakamahi whai rawa te wete atu.

Ka whakatakoto te Wiki 3 o te akoranga Mātauranga AI mō ngā Whānau i te muna hei take papatipu, take papatipu hoki, ehara i te raruraru whaiaro, ka hoatu i tētahi reo tahi mō te whānau ki te whakatau tahi.

Utukore, raihana tuwhera, pānui i te pūtirotiro: https://robertmccallnz.github.io/ai-literacy-for-families/

## Panui 3 — Ko tō taiohi te hua

**Taupatupatu:** Te tango i te aro me ngā raraunga o ngā taiohi hei mahi kāore he utu.

### Twitter/X
Ko ia panekeneke, ia tono, ia kōrero "utukore" ki te poro kōrero he raraunga whakangungu.

Kei te mahi rātou. Kāore rātou e utua ana.

Ko te Wiki 2 o te akoranga e whakaatu ana i tēnei, e tuku ana hoki i te reo ki ngā whānau ki te kōrero mō reira.

robertmccallnz.github.io/ai-literacy-for-families

### Pukamata
Anei tētahi mea kāore te nuinga o ngā kōrero "haumarutanga ipurangi" e kī ana:

Ehara tō taiohi i te kaiwhakamahi noa o ēnei taputapu AI. Kei te whakangungua e rātou. He mahi kāore he utu ia tono, ia whakatika, ia hāora o te aro hei whakapiki i te utu o te hua a tētahi atu.

Ehara tēnei i te hapa. Koia te tauira pakihi.

Ka hoatu e te Wiki 2 o tēnei akoranga Mātauranga AI mō ngā Whānau utukore tētahi ara mārama ki te mātua me te taiohi ki te kite, ki te kōrero, kāore he whakaako, kāore he whakaohorere.

## Panui 4 — Ehara te whakatoihara i te hapa, he pukapuka pūtea

**Taupatupatu:** Ko te whakatoihara taurangi he hua papatipu nā wai i hanga, i whakatupu, i whakangungu i te AI.

### Twitter/X
Ko te kōrero "kei te whakatoihara te taurangi" he āhua aituā.

Ehara. He whakaata i te tangata nā wai te kamupene, nā wai i whakangungu, nā wai te raraunga utu iti hei tango.

I te Wiki 4, ka akona ngā whānau ki te pānui i taua pukapuka pūtea.

robertmccallnz.github.io/ai-literacy-for-families

### Pukamata
Kia kī mai tētahi kamupene "kei te mahi mātou ki te whakaiti i te whakatoihara o tā mātou AI," pātai koi atu:

Te whakatoihara mō tā wai? Nā wai i hanga? I whakangungua ki te raraunga a wai? I hokona ki ēhea kiritaki?

Ehara te whakatoihara i te hapa hangarau hei whakatika. He hua tūmau o te mana pupuri me ngā mahi e hiahiatia ana.

Ka hoatu e te Wiki 4 o tēnei akoranga Mātauranga AI mō ngā Whānau utukore tētahi anga ki ngā mātua me ngā taiohi ki te pātai tahi i ēnei pātai, kāore e hiahiatia he tohu mātauranga rorohiko.

Pānuihia te akoranga katoa i tō pūtirotiro, utukore: https://robertmccallnz.github.io/ai-literacy-for-families/

Ka whakatauhia te whakatoihara e ngā hanganga mana pupuri, ngā whakaihiihi pūtea, ngā ritenga tango raraunga whakangungu, me ngā mākete kiritaki. Ki te tirohia hei raruraru hangarau anake, ka huna ngā whakatau tōrangapū me te ōhanga i hua ai.
