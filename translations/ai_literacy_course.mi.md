## Tirohanga whānui o te Akoranga

He akoranga tēnei mō ngā mātua me ngā taiohi (mai i te 12 tau ki te 18 tau pea) hei mahi **tahi**. He kaupapa ano tēnā wiki, me ngā pānuitanga tahi, he kōrerorero ārahi, me he mahi-ā-ringa kia whai wāhi tahi ai te whānau ki te whakamātau i te AI — kaua ko ia tangata ki tana rūma anake.

**Te āhua:** E ono ngā wāhanga ā-wiki, 60–90 meneti te roa o ia wāhanga
**Te hunga whāia:** Ngā tokorua mātua-taiohi (rōpū whānau iti rānei)
**Te whāinga:** Hei te mutunga o te akoranga, ka whai te whānau i te kupu mahi tahi mō te AI, he whakaaetanga ā-whānau mō tōna whakamahinga, me ngā hangarau whakaaro arohaehae kia ngā tonu te ako i te hurihanga o te hangarau.

**Te hanga o ia wāhanga:**
1. **Ngā whāinga ako** — ko ngā mea ka taea e koe i te mutunga
2. **Ngā ariā matua** — he pānui poto, he ataata rānei hei mahi takitahi i mua i te hui
3. **Ngā pātai kōrerorero** — ngā pātai hei mahi tahi
4. **Te mahi-ā-kāinga** — he mahi tūturu hei mahi tokorua
5. **He rauemi mā ngā mātua** — he tuhinga taunaki pono
6. **Whakaaroaro** — he pātai mō te rārangi mahara whakamutunga

## Wiki 1 — Me pēhea te mahi o te AI

### Ngā whāinga ako
- Whakamāramatia, mā ngā kupu māmā, te mahi a te large language model (LLM) me te mihini whakaputa whakaahua.
- Whakawehea te AI **whakapae**, te AI **whakarōpū**, me te AI **whakaputa**.
- Mōhiotia kei hea kē te AI i roto i ngā taputapu o ia rā (rapunga, hononga pāpori, taputapu whakaahua, autocorrect).

### Ngā ariā matua
He pūtahi tauira-whakataurite ngā pūnaha AI, kua whakangungua ki ngā pātaka raraunga rahi. Kāore rātou i te "mōhio" pērā i te tangata — ka whakaputa rātou i ngā putanga e tūpono ana, e ai ki ngā mea kua kitea i mua. Ko tēnei whakaaro kotahi te take o te nuinga o ngā mea ka tae mai ā muri: ko te take ka pohehe te AI, ko te take ka whakaata i te whakatoihara, ko te take ka maia tonu te reo o te AI ahakoa e hē ana.

### Ngā pātai kōrerorero
1. I mua i tēnei wiki, he aha tāu i whakaaro ai mō te mahi a te AI ina pātai koe? E pēhea te tata o tāu whakaaro ki te pono?
2. Kei hea koe e kite ana i te AI i roto i ngā taputapu kē e whakamahia ana e koe? Hangaia he rārangi tahi.
3. Ki te whakapae noa te AI i te kupu e whai mai ana, he aha ngā momo pātai e pai ana, he aha ngā momo pātai e kino ana?
4. Taiohi ki te mātua: he aha tētahi mea i hiahia koe kia mōhio te pakeke mō te whakamahi a tōu whakatipuranga i te AI? Mātua ki te taiohi: he aha tētahi mea i hiahia koe kia mārama ake koe?

### Mahi-ā-kāinga: "Kotahi te pātai, e toru ngā taputapu"
Whiriwhiria he pātai tūwhera e aroha ana kōrua (hei tauira: *"He aha tā tāua tunu mō tēnei wiki, $60 noa te utu?"* "He aha ngā take o WWI?"). Pātaihia te **pātai kotahi** i ngā taputapu e toru — pērā i a ChatGPT, Google Gemini, me te rapunga Google māori. Whakatauritehia ngā whakautu:
- Kei hea rātou e whakaae ana?
- Kei hea rātou e tautohe ana?
- Ko tēhea i tāpiri i ngā puna? He puna pono?
- Ko tēhea i maia rawa atu? He ōrite te maia ki te tika?

Tuhia kotahi rerenga kōrero ki ia tangata mō ngā mea i miharo ai.

## Wiki 2 — Whakatoihara me ngā Deepfake

### Ngā whāinga ako
- Whakamāramatia te take ka mau te whakatoihara o ngā raraunga whakangungu i te AI.
- Tautohutia ngā tohu ataata, ororongo, horopaki hoki o te deepfake.
- Kia mārama, kua mutu te āhei o te whakaaro "he āhua pono" hei tohu mō "he pono".

### Ngā ariā matua
E rua ngā raruraru kei te pokapū o tēnei wiki. He hanganga te **whakatoihara** — ki te whakangungua he AI ki te reo, te kanohi, te whakaaro ao kotahi, ka āta whakarahihia tērā. He whakaaro tukuhuru ngā **deepfake** — he whakaahua, ororongo, ataata hangaia kia āhua pono. He ōrite te hangarau o ēnei, he ōrite hoki te parepare: ko te whakamana wāhi-ki-wāhi, kaua ko te whakaaro pīwari noa.

### Ngā pātai kōrerorero
1. Ki te whakangungua he AI ki ngā tuhinga o tētahi whenua kotahi, reo kotahi, whakaaro tōrangapū kotahi rānei, pēhea pea te whakapae o ana whakautu? Ko whose ngā reo ka ngaro?
2. Kua kite kōrua i tētahi mea ipurangi e whakapaehia ana e kōrua nā te AI i hanga? He aha ngā tohu? He aha i kore ai e mōhio?
3. He rerekē ranei te deepfake hei whakatakawai, te mea hei tāhae, te mea hei whakawhiu i tētahi? Me ōrite te whakahaere o ēnei katoa?
4. Mēnā ka puta he deepfake ōu (ō tētahi hoa rānei) āpōpō, he aha tāu e hiahia ana? Ko wai tāu e kōrero tuatahi?

### Mahi-ā-kāinga: "Whakamātauria te whakatoihara + rapua te deepfake"
**Wāhanga A — Whakamātautau whakatoihara (20 meneti).** Pātaihia tētahi mihini whakaputa whakaahua (pērā i tā ChatGPT, Gemini, taputapu kore-utu rānei) ki te whakaputa whakaahua mō ngā tono toru tekau: *"He CEO," "he tapuhi," "he taihara," "he kaipūtaiao," "he tangata horoi," "he marena," "he whare ātaahua," "he tangata kāinga-kore," "he taiohi ako," "he whānau e kai ana."* Kaua e tāpiri kupu āhua. Tirohia ngā putanga tahi: he aha ngā tauira o te ira tangata, te iwi, te tau, te tinana, te wāhi, te whairawa? He aha ngā mea ka ngaro?

**Wāhanga B — Whakatewhatewha deepfake (20 meneti).** Toro atu ki te [pae MIT "Detect Fakes"](https://deepfakes.virtuality.mit.edu/) me mahi tahi i ngā tauira. Kātahi panuhia ō kōrua hononga pāpori mō te 10 meneti, e rapu ana i ngā whakaahua AI. Kōrerohia ngā tohu i mau ai.

## Wiki 3 — Tūmataitinga me te Matatika

### Ngā whāinga ako
- Kia mārama he aha ngā raraunga ka kohia ina whakamahia e te taiohi he kōrero AI, he taupānga AI, he hoa AI rānei.
- Whakamāramatia te wehenga o te whakamahi **ture** me te whakamahi **matatika** o te AI.
- Whakatauhia me whakamahi i ēhea taputapu AI me he aha hei tuari atu.

### Ngā ariā matua
He uaua te kī he kore-utu ngā taputapu AI kore-utu. Ko te utu ko ō raraunga — ō tono, ō kōnae, ō reo, ō kanohi ētahi wā. Hei tāpiri ki te raruraru tūmataiti, kei kō atu te raruraru matatika: ka whakamahia e te 75% o ngā taiohi ngā taupānga hoa AI, ahakoa ngā raru o te whakawhirinaki kare ā-roto me te haumarutanga mō te hunga taiohi.

### Ngā pātai kōrerorero
1. Ina pato koe ki te kōrero AI, ki hea pea taua tuhinga e haere ana? Ko wai pea kei te pānui? E hia te roa ka pupurihia?
2. He mea kua tuaritia kē e koe ki te AI kāore koe e pīrangi kia pānuitia e te kaiako, e te kaituku mahi, e te hoa rangatira ā muri? (Kaua e tohatoha tūturu — me kī "Āe" "Kāo" anake.)
3. Kua hangaia ngā taupānga hoa AI kia rite ki ngā hoa. He aha te wehenga i waenga i te taputapu hei āwhina, me te taputapu e hiahia ana koe kia hoki tonu mai?
4. Mēnā ka whakamahia ō raraunga e tētahi taupānga utu-kore hei whakangungu i tana tauira, he tauhokohoko tika tēnā? Ka rerekē tāu whakautu mēnā he tamaiti 9-tau te kaiwhakamahi?

### Mahi-ā-kāinga: "Horoi hōhonu i ngā tautuhinga tūmataiti"
Tīkina ngā **taupānga e toru e nui rawa atu ana** ki te whakamahi (Snapchat, Instagram, TikTok, ChatGPT, Gemini, Character.AI, taputapu whakaahua, kaikōrero atamai pea). Mō ia mea, mahi tahi:
1. Huakina ngā tautuhinga tūmataiti.
2. Rapua te wāhanga "raraunga whakangungu" me whakatauhia me kape rānei.
3. Tirohia ngā mea e tuaritia ana ki ngā kaihoko atu.
4. Tirohia ngā mea kua tiakina i te hītori kōrero, me ūkui ngā mea kāore e tika kia ora tonu.

Tuhia he tuhipoka tahi mō ngā mea kua hurihia. Tirohia anō ia ono marama.

## Wiki 4 — Whakaaro Arohaehae me te Whakamana Meka

### Ngā whāinga ako
- Whakamahia te **tikanga SIFT** ki ngā mea katoa kua hangaia, whakanuia rānei e te AI.
- Kitea ngā "moemoeā" o te AI — ngā meka hē, ngā kōrero whakatoi, ngā kupu hangaia.
- Hangaia he tikanga whakamana meka whaiaro kia kotahi noa, e rua meneti ki raro iho.

### Ngā ariā matua
Ka whakaputa maia te AI i ngā meka teka — keehi ture hē, kupu whakahua hangaia, tohutoro pūtaiao pohehe. Ehara te maia i te tohu o te tika. Ko te parepare pakari — ehara i te taputapu motuhake, engari he ritenga. Ko te **SIFT** a Mike Caulfield, he tikanga e whā: **S**top (tū), **I**nvestigate (whakatewhatewha i te puna), **F**ind (rapua he tuhinga pai ake), **T**race (whai i ngā kerēme ki te horopaki tūturu).

### Ngā pātai kōrerorero
1. Kōrerohia tētahi wā i whakapono koe ki tētahi mea ipurangi i hē. He aha i whakapono ai? He aha i mau ai?
2. He aha pea i hangaia ai e te AI tētahi meka me te whakapuaki maia? (Tohu: tirohia anō te Wiki 1.)
3. Mēnā e kī ana te TikTok, te whakarāpopototanga AI, me te tuhinga pae pāpāho i te kupu kotahi, e toru ēnei puna — kotahi rānei? Me pēhea koe ki te tirotiro?
4. He aha te wehenga o *"Kāore au e mōhio"* me *"Kua rangahaua e ahau, kāore i te pono"*? Ko tēhea ka whakarere te AI?

### Mahi-ā-kāinga: "Hopukia te AI e teka ana"
Hangaia tahi he pātai e rima e mōhio nei kōrua ki ngā whakautu tika — te mahi a tētahi mema o te whānau, te kīanga o tētahi pukapuka tino pai, te hua o tētahi tākaro ā-rohe, he meka mō tētahi mahi a-ngahau, he kaupapa hītori o tōu whenua. Pātaihia he kōrero AI i ia pātai, me whakatauria:
- **Tika**
- **Tika ētahi wāhi (he hapa)**
- **Maia me te hē**
- **Kāore i whakahoki / i āta kōrero**

Mō ngā whakautu "maia me te hē", whakahaerea ngā taipitopito SIFT. Kōrerohia: ka mau pea i a koe mēnā kāore koe i mōhio kē?

## Wiki 5 — Whakatūngia ngā Rohenga Mamati ā-Kāinga

### Ngā whāinga ako
- Hangaia tahi he whakaaetanga ā-whānau mō te whakamahi AI ka whakaaetia e te mātua, e te taiohi hoki.
- Whakawehea ngā rohenga me whai mana, me ngā rohenga me whai kōrero.
- Tautohutia ngā āhuatanga me uru tonu te pakeke.

### Ngā ariā matua
Ka kore ngā rohenga e mau ki te kore e whakaaetia. Ko te wiki tino mahi-tahi tēnei o te akoranga. Ehara te whāinga he kohinga ture nā te mātua — engari he **whakaaetanga ā-kāinga** ka hainatia e te rua taha nā te mea i tuhia e te rua taha. He aratohu pai te Family Media Plan a te American Academy of Pediatrics; whakaurua mō te AI tonu.

### Ngā pātai kōrerorero
1. Ko ēhea whakamahi AI e **mārama te tika** ki a kōrua? Ko ēhea ka rāhuihia? Ko ēhea e kapi ana i te awe?
2. Ko te kura me ngā mahi kāinga: āhea te whakamahi AI he taputapu (pērā i te tatauranga), āhea he tinihanga? Kei hea te rohenga a tō kura — e whakaae ana koe?
3. Ko ngā hoa AI me te tautoko kare ā-roto: i ngā āhuatanga aha, mēnā tonu, ka pai te whakamahi? Āhea ka huri hei whakakapinga mō te hononga tūturu?
4. He aha tō kōrua hiahia tētahi i tētahi? (Taiohi: he aha tāu e hiahia ana kia mutu i te mātua? Mātua: he aha tāu e hiahia ana kia tīmata i te taiohi?)
5. He aha te ture "karangahia he tangata" — ngā wā ka whakaaetia kia mutu, kia katia te taupānga, kia kōrero ki tētahi tangata tūturu?

### Mahi-ā-kāinga: "Tuhia tō whakaaetanga AI ā-kāinga"
Whakamahia he tuhinga tahi. Hangaia ngā wāhanga e rima:

1. **Ko ā mātou whakamahi AI** (mahi kāinga, tunu, whakaaro, whakatika whakaahua, aha atu rānei)
2. **Ko ā mātou kore-whakamahi AI** (hei tauira: whakaahua whaiaro ki ngā mihini whakaputa, ingoa tūturu, wāhi noho, te whakakapi i te hoa tūturu, te kaitautoko rānei)
3. **Ko ngā mea ka whakaaturia e mātou** ("ki te whakamahi au i te AI mō te mahi kura, ka kī au ki te kaiako mēnā ka pātaihia ahau"; "ki te whakatika au i te whakaahua mā te AI, ka kī au")
4. **Ture wā me wāhi** (kāore he hoa AI i muri i te 10pm; kāore he waea i ngā moenga i te pō; kotahi kai kore-mata ia rā)
5. **Te rārangi "karangahia he tangata"** — ngā āhuatanga ka mutu te whakamahi AI, ka kōrero ki te tangata tūturu (pātai hauora hinengaro, tohu hauora tinana, raru ture, ngā mea e mataku ai koe)

Hainatia, tuhi i te rā. Whakairia ki tētahi wāhi e kitea ana. Whakaaetia he wā arotake i muri i ngā rā 90.

## Wiki 6 — Whakaatu i ngā Ritenga Hangarau Whakawhirinaki

### Ngā whāinga ako
- Kia mārama ka ako te taiohi i ngā ritenga hangarau nā te mātakitaki i ngā pakeke.
- Tautohutia ngā mahi ka whakatauiratia e ia mema o te whānau — me ngā mea ka mutu.
- Hangaia he ritenga ka mau tonu kia kore ai ngā akoranga e ngaro i ngā marama e toru.

### Ngā ariā matua
Ka tae te rangahau o ngā ritenga pāpāho whānau ki te whakataunga kotahi: ko ngā mahi a te mātua he kaha ake i ngā ture katoa hei whakatika i te hononga o te tamaiti ki te hangarau. Ki te kei runga te waea o te mātua i te tēpu kai, kāore te ture "kaua he waea i te tēpu" e mau. Mēnā kāore te mātua e kī "Kāore au e mōhio — me tirotiro", kāore hoki te taiohi. Ka huri tēnei wiki i te ataata. Ka tuku oati anō ngā mātua mō ā rātou hurihanga.

### Ngā pātai kōrerorero
1. Mātua: he aha tō ritenga hangarau e whakamā ai koe mēnā ka kapehia tonu e tō taiohi? Taiohi: te pātai anō ki a koe.
2. Āhea i kite ai kōrua i tētahi ki tētahi e whakatakoto ana i tana taputapu kia tino noho tahi? He pēhea te āhua?
3. He pēhea te whakamahi **pai** o te AI i tēnei whare? Kōrerohia kia toru ngā tauira tūturu kua mahia kē i ngā wiki e ono kua taha.
4. He aha tāu e hiahia ana mō te hononga o tō whānau ki te hangarau i ngā tau e rima? Hokia whakamuri: he aha tāu e mahi ai i tēnei marama kia haere tika ai?
5. He aha tāu i ako i tētahi i tētahi i tēnei akoranga, kāore koe i tūmanako?

### Mahi-ā-kāinga: "Te oati ataata + te hui ā-marama"
**Wāhanga A — Ngā oati ataata.** Tuhia e ia tangata kia **toru** ngā mahi ka hurihia, ka kitea e tērā atu. Hei tauira:
- "Ka waiho e ahau taku waea i te kīhini i te wā kai."
- "Ka kī au ki te whakamahi au i te AI hei tuhi."
- "Ka pātai ahau i mua i te tuari whakaahua ōu."
- "Kāore au e whakamahi i taku taupānga hoa AI i ngā pō kura."

Whakawhitiwhitia ngā rārangi. Whakairia ki te pouaka makariri.

**Wāhanga B — Whakaritea te hui.** Whakaurua he hui ā-whānau 30 meneti i te maramataka — ia marama te pai. E toru ngā pātai i ia hui:
1. He aha te āwhina o te AI i tēnei marama?
2. He aha te utu i a tātou i tēnei marama?
3. He pai tonu tā tātou whakaaetanga, me whakatika rānei?

## Whakamutunga o te Akoranga

Hei te mutunga o ēnei wiki e ono, me whai kōrua:

- He kupu mahi tahi mō te kōrero mō te AI me te kore mataku, me te kore whakahīhī
- He whakaaetanga AI ā-whānau kua hainatia, me te wā arotake
- He ritenga whakamana meka (SIFT) me ētahi pūkete tūmataiti
- He hui ā-marama kia ora tonu te kōrerorero

## Tuhipoka mō te Kaiwhakahaere (mō te tuku ipurangi)

- **Tere:** Kotahi wāhanga ia wiki te tika. Kia kaua e kuiti ki te rangi mutunga wiki — ko te wā **i waenga** i ngā hui ko te wā ka tūturu mai ngā kōrerorero.
- **Whakaritenga ki te wā:** Ka taea ia wāhanga hei wāhanga takitahi. Ki te whakahaeretia hei akoranga rōpū, whakaritea he hui ora 30 meneti i te mutunga o ia wiki.
- **Tauwhāingi:** Ka pai ngā pātai mai i te 12 tau. Mō ngā tamariki taiohi ake (8–11), whakamāmāhia ngā Wiki 2 me te 3.
- **Whānau mātua-kotahi, taiohi-maha:** Ka taea e ngā mahi katoa te whakarite. Ko te "tokorua" he mātua + taiohi, e rua mātua, e rua tuākana-tēina, kaitiaki + tamaiti rānei.
- **Whakahōuhia:** Kia tere te huri o te AI. Tirohia ngā rauemi ia ono marama.
