# AI Mātauranga mō ngā Mātua me ngā Rangatahi — Kete Akoranga Substack

Kei roto i tēnei kete ngā **waru tuhinga Substack kua rite ki te tāhurihia**: kotahi tuhinga whakatūwhera, ono akoranga ā-wiki, me tetahi tuhinga whakakapi. Kua tāruatia ia tuhinga i roto i ngā tikanga e pai ana ki te ētita Substack — tīpoka tāpui, tūhono whakaaro, rārangi, me ngā hononga māmā (kāore he HTML tōpuni).

**Me pēhea te whakamahi i tēnei kete:**

1. Ka wehewehe ia tuhinga ki te rārangi pārāone (`---`).
2. Tāhurihia ngā mea katoa mai i te taitara o te tuhinga ki te `---` e whai ake ana.
3. Tāhurihia ki roto i te ētita Substack — ka tika ngā upoko, tāpui, kōpito, tūhono whakaaro, rārangi, me ngā hononga.
4. He āhua whakaaro mō te whakaputanga: kotahi tuhinga ia wiki, ka whakaritea mō te rā kotahi ia wiki kia mōhio ngā kairapu ka tae ia wā.
5. Ko ngā tūtohu kaupeka mō ia tuhinga kei raro o ia wāhanga, i roto i ngā kuputūāhua.

Ko te tuhinga whakatūwhera he tuhinga tūmatiti kore utu hei tō kairapu. Ko ngā akoranga wiki ono ka taea te tuku kore utu, utu rānei — ka pai tōna pānuitanga āhea atu rānei.

## TUHINGA 0 — Whakatūwhera: He Akoranga 6-Wiki hei āwhina i tō Whānau ki te Māramatanga mō AI

**Upoko Tautika:** He raupeka wiki kore utu mō ngā mātua me ngā rangatahi hei mahi tahi. Kotahi kaupeka ia wiki, kotahi kōrero, kotahi mea hei whakamātau ki te kāinga.

---

Mēnā he rangatahi kei tō whare, kua āpōpō te AI. Kei roto i ō rātou hua rapu, ō rātou kōrero rōpū, ō rātou mahi kura, ō rātou ētita whakaahua, ō rātou tūtohutanga waiata — ā, mō tata te tikanga o ngā rangatahi taiohi o Amerika — i roto i ō rātou kōrero ā-rā ki ētahi whakaaro rōpū.

Ko te nuinga o ngā mātua e kōrero ana au ki a rātou e rongo ana i ngā mea e rua i te wā kotahi. Ko te tuatahi ko te tere o te AI e neke ana ake i tō rātou āheinga ki te aro. Ko te tuarua ko ngā tohutohu o nāianei — "kōrero ki ō tamariki mōna" — e whakaaro ana he māramatanga mō tēnā kaupeka ō rātou kāore ō rātou.

I hangaia tēnei raupeka 6-wiki i runga i tēnā āputa. Ehara i te kōrero whakamātautau. He kōrero hanganga e mahi tahi ai koe **me** tō rangatahi, kaua ko ia. Ko ia wiki, kotahi kaupeka, kotahi rārangi pātai hei mahi tahi, me kotahi mea ngāwari hei mahi ki te kāinga i mua i te tānga o te tuhinga e whai ake ana.

### Ngā mea kei te heke mai

- **Wiki 1 —** Me pēhea tonu te mahi o te AI (te whakautu āniwhaniwha, whakaora)
- **Wiki 2 —** Ngā pakiaro me ngā deepfake
- **Wiki 3 —** Tūmataiti me ngā tikanga
- **Wiki 4 —** Whakaaro māhio me te tirotiro i ngā kōrero
- **Wiki 5 —** Te whakarite i ngā rohe matihiko ā-whare
- **Wiki 6 —** Te whakaata i ngā tikanga hangarau pai

### Mō wai tēnei

- Ngā mātua o ngā tamariki e tata ana ki ngā tau 12 ki te 18
- Ngā rangatahi e pai ake ana ki te māramatanga o te hangarau i te whakamātautau mōna
- Ngā mātua kotahi, ngā whare e rua ngā mātua, ngā kaitiaki, ngā tīpuna — ko tēhea rānei e whai ana i tētahi rangatahi me tētahi pakiaka e pai ana ki te whakarite kotahi hāora ia wiki

### Me pēhea te mahi

Pānuitia ia tuhinga motuhake, kātahi ka noho ki raro mō ngā miniti 60–90 o tērā wiki hei mahi i ngā pātai kōrero me te mahi ā-kāinga. Ko ērā anake.

Ā te mutunga o ngā wiki ono, ka whai kōrero ā-reo kotahi, he whakaaetanga AI ā-whare i hainahia, me tētahi tirotiro ā-marama kua whakaritea. Ka tonu te huri o te hangarau. Ko te tikanga o te kōrero i ēnei āhuatanga tahi ko tērā e taea tonutia.

Ohia mai ki ia wiki ki tō pouaka kōrero. Ka tānga te akoranga tuatahi i tērā wiki.

*Ngā tūtohu: ngā mātua, AI, mātauranga matihiko, hangarau, whānau, rangatahi*

## TUHINGA 1 — Wiki 1: Me Pēhea Tonu te Mahi o te AI

**Upoko Tautika:** Mukua te mōrearea me te māharahara tūāhua, ka māmā ake te kōrero mō te AI.

---

I mua i tō kōrero ki tō rangatahi mō te pakiaro o te AI, ngā deepfake, tūmataiti rānei, me whai e kōrua he whakautu mō te pātai tino māmā: **he aha tonu tēnei mea e mahi ana?**

Ko te nuinga o ngā tāngata — ōkawa ngā pakiaka — e whakaaro ana he mea ngaro ake ai te AI i tō rātou māramatanga. Ko tērā mōrearea ko tērā e wehi ai, ā, ko tērā anō e tino whakapono ai. Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki ko te whakamāramatia.

### Ko te whakaaro kotahi e whakamārama ana i ngā mea katoa

> Ko ngā pūnaha AI he miihini tauira-tūtaki i whakaakona ki runga i ngā raraunga nui. Kāore rātou e "mōhio" ana ki ngā mea pērā ki tētahi tangata — ka puta ō rātou hua he mea rite ki ngā tauira i kite rātou i mua.

Ko tēnā rerenga kotahi e whakamārama ana i ngā mea katoa e whai ake ana i tēnei akoranga. He aha te take ka "tīpakohia" ai e te AI. He aha te take ka akona ai ōna pakiaro. He aha te take ka taea ai tōna tino māia me tōna hē tino. Puritia ki tō pēkē tuarā.

### Ko ngā āheitanga kei tō ringa ā te mutunga o tēnei wiki

- Whakamāramatia ki ngā kupu māmā he aha tonu te mahi a tētahi chatbot me tētahi pūnaha hanga whakaahua
- Mātatohua te rereketanga i waenga i te AI e **matapae** ana i tētahi mea, e **whakarōpū** ana i tētahi mea, e **hanga** ana i tētahi mea
- Kite ai i te AI kua tūtū kē i roto i ngā tono e whakamahia ana ā-rā — rapu, ngā puna pāpāho, autocorrect, ētita whakaahua

### Ngā pātai e whā hei mahi tahi

1. I mua i tēnei wiki, he aha ō whakaaro o ia tangata mō tā te AI e *mahi* ana i a koe e pātai ana? He ōrite rānei ki te reality?
2. I kite koe i te AI e puta mai ana i ngā tono e whakamahi ana koe? Whakaritea he rārangi tahi.
3. Mēnā he miihini matapae kupu anake te AI, he aha ngā pātai e pai ana — ā, he aha ngā pātai e kino ana?
4. Rangatahi ki te mātua: he aha tētahi mea e hiahia ana koe ko ngā pakiaka ki te māramatanga o te whakamahinga o tō reanga ki te AI? Mātua ki te rangatahi: he aha tētahi mea e hiahia ana koe ki te māramatanga pai ake?

### Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki: Ko te pātai kotahi, ko ngā taputapu e toru

Kōwhirihia tētahi pātai māmā e pāpono tika ana ki ā kōrua: pērā i *"He aha tā tāua tunu i tēnei wiki mō te $60?"* rānei *"He aha ngā take o WWI?"*

Nā, pātaihia te **pātai kotahi** ki ngā taputapu e toru:

- ChatGPT (Claude, Gemini rānei — kōwhirihia tētahi chatbot)
- Tētahi atu chatbot, rerekē i te tuatahi
- Tētahi rapu Google māmā

Whakatauritea ngā whakautu. E ārite ana rānei? E rereketia ana rānei? Ko tēhea ka tūhono i ōna puna, ā, he puna tūturu rānei? Ko tēhea i tino māia ake — ā, ko tēhea te māia, ko tēhea te tika?

Tuhia he rerenga kotahi ia tangata mō tō rātou ohorere.

### Mō ngā mātua e hiahia ana ki te hōhonu ake

- [Day of AI & Common Sense Media — "He aha te AI mō ngā Whānau" taputapu](https://dayofai.org/news/day-of-ai-and-common-sense-media-launch-essential-toolkit-for-parents-and-families-talking-to-kids-about-ai-privacy-fairness-and-responsibility): ngā whakamāramatanga poto e tika ana mō ngā kōrero ā-whānau.
- [MIT RAISE](https://raise.mit.edu/): te pokapū MIT mō ngā rauemi AI mātauranga.
- [The Common Parent — Aratohu Mātua mō AI](https://www.thecommonparent.com/screen-sense-guide-to-ai): tirohanga māia, kāore he wehi.
- [Elements of AI](https://www.elements-of-ai.com/): he akoranga kuhu kore utu nō te Whare Wānanga o Helsinki — tūtohu mēnā e hiahia ana tō rangatahi ki te haere ake.

### Whakaaro kapi

Tuhia e ia tangata tētahi rerenga: *"I mua i tēnei wiki i whakaaro au ko te AI ___; nō nāianei e whakaaro ana au ko ia ___.\"* Waihotia ki tētahi wāhi e kite ai kōrua. I tērā wiki ka titiro tāua ki ngā huarahi e rua e rangahaiahia ana te AI — ngā pakiaro me ngā deepfake.

## TUHINGA 2 — Wiki 2: Ngā Pakiaro me ngā Deepfake

**Upoko Tautika:** Ngā raru e rua, kotahi ārai — me tētahi mahi ngahau (tino tūkinotia tētahi) mō te mutunga wiki.

---

I tērā wiki i whakaritea e tāua ko te AI he miihini tauira-tūtaki, ehara i te orakuru ngaro. Ko tēnā kōrero kotahi e hura ana i ngā kaupeka e rua o tēnei wiki.

Ko te **pakiaro** ko tērā ka puta ina akona ana ngā tauira mai i tētahi wāhanga iti o te ao. Ko ngā **deepfake** ko tērā ka whakamahia ana e tētahi te hangarau kotahi hei hōhā i te reality. Ko ā rāua e rua ka whakaaro ana i te mihini kotahi, ā, ko ā rāua e rua ka hiahia ana ki te ārai kotahi: mutu te whakapono ki tō ngākau, tiimata ki te tirotiro ōrite.

### Ko ngā āheitanga kei tō ringa ā te mutunga o tēnei wiki

- Whakamāramatia he aha te take ka tīpū ai ngā pakiaro mai i ngā raraunga whakaako
- Kitea ngā tohu ataata, oro, me te horopaki e whakaaro ana ko tētahi deepfake
- Māramatia ko "āhua tūturu" kāore anō hei pūnaha hou mō "he tūturu"

### Ngā pātai e whā hei mahi tahi

1. Mēnā i whakaakona tētahi AI i runga i ngā tuhinga nō tētahi whenua, tētahi reo, tētahi tirohanga tōrangapū rānei, me pēhea te huri o ōna whakautu? Ko ā wai reo ka ngaro?
2. I kite kōrua i tētahi mea i runga ipurangi ōkawa ko AI tona hanganga? He aha i tohua ai koe — rānei he aha i kore ai?
3. He rereketanga ōhea i waenga i tētahi deepfake i hangaia mō te pūkiri, mō tētahi tinihanga, mō te whakarereke tangata rānei? Me whakaarotia ōrite rānei ngā mea e toru?
4. Mēnā ka puta tētahi deepfake ōu (o tō hoa rānei) ā-ipurangi āpōpō, he aha tāu e hiahia ana kia puta? Ko wai tāu ka kōrero tuatahi?

### Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki: Arotake pakiaro + kimi deepfake

He mahi e rua. E tata ana ki ngā miniti 40 i te katoa.

**Wāhanga A — Arotake pakiaro (20 miniti)**

Huakina tētahi pūnaha hanga whakaahua tahi (taputapu whakaahua ChatGPT, Gemini, kē rānei). Tono ia ki te hanga whakaahua mō ēnei tono tekau, **kāore he āhua tohu i tāpirihia**:

- Tētahi Kaiwhakahaere
- Tētahi Tapuhi
- Tētahi kaiara
- Tētahi kaiputaiao
- Tētahi tangata horoi whare
- Tētahi mārena
- Tētahi whare ātaahua
- Tētahi tangata kāinga kore
- Tētahi rangatahi e ako ana
- Tētahi whānau e kai ana

Nā titiro ai ki ngā hua e tekau tahi. He aha ngā tauira e puta ana i roto i te ira, i te iwi, i te pakiaka, i te āhua tinana, i te wāhi, i te rawa? Ko ā wai ora ka ngaro katoa?

**Wāhanga B — Kimi deepfake (20 miniti)**

Toro atu ki [MIT's "Detect Fakes" paetukutuku mātauranga pāpāho](https://deepfakes.virtuality.mit.edu/) ā, mahi tahi i ā rātou tauira. Kātahi whakangahau ngā miniti 10 ki roto i ō rāua ao pāpāho rite — ka kimikimi tūturu i ngā whakaahua i hangaia e AI. Whakatauritea ngā kōrero mō tērā i tohua ai rāua.

### Mō ngā mātua e hiahia ana ki te hōhonu ake

- [MIT — Mātauranga Pāpāho i roto i te Wā o ngā Deepfake](https://deepfakes.virtuality.mit.edu/)
- [Parents Pass It On — Me pēhea te whakamāramatanga o te Pakiaro AI ki ngā Rangatahi](https://www.parentspassiton.com/blog/how-to-explain-ai-bias-to-teens-in-simple-terms)
- [Common Sense Media — Deepfakes, Whakahē me te Kōrero Hē](https://www.commonsensemedia.org/articles/deepfakes-distrust-and-disinformation-the-ai-powered-information-apocalypse)
- [Algorithmic Justice League](https://www.ajl.org/) — te rōpū a Joy Buolamwini, me ngā tauira ao-tūturu pai rawa atu o te pakiaro tūnga kanohi

### Whakaaro kapi

Ia tangata: ingoa tētahi pūrongo pāpāho i kite ai koe i tēnei wiki e hiahia ana koe ki te arohia anō. I tērā wiki: ko hea tonu e haere ana ō raraunga i a koe e kōrero ana ki tētahi AI.

## TUHINGA 3 — Wiki 3: Tūmataiti me ngā Tikanga

**Upoko Tautika:** He iti te utu o te AI kore utu. Ko te utu ko ngā raraunga — ā, mō ngā rangatahi, he maha ake ōkawa.

---

E tata ana ki te tikanga o ngā rangatahi taiohi o Amerika kua whakamahi i tētahi chatbot hoa AI. Ehara i te tatauranga o ngā taitara. Ko tēnā te rangatahi ōrite.

He kore utu ēnei taputapu ki te kōwhai whakamahi. Ehara i te kore utu tūturu. Ko te utu ko ngā raraunga — ō tono, ō whakaahua tāhuri, ōkawa tō reo. Ā, i runga ake i tērā pātai tūmataiti ka tū tērā pātai tikanga kino ake: i hangaia ngā hoa AI kia rite ai ki ngā hoa, ā, ko te tauira pakiuara ka whiwhi utu i te pupuhi i ngā kaiwhakamahi ki te hoki mai. Mō raro i te 18, ko tērā tōpūtanga he mōrearea tūturu.

### Ko ngā āheitanga kei tō ringa ā te mutunga o tēnei wiki

- Māramatia ngā raraunga ka kohia i a koe e whakamahi ana i tētahi chatbot AI rangatahi
- Wehewehea te whakamahinga *ture* me te *tikanga* o te AI
- Mahia ngā kōwhiringa mōhio mō ngā taputapu e whakamahia ana me ngā mea ka tukuna

### Ngā pātai e whā hei mahi tahi

1. I a koe e tāuru ana i tētahi mea ki tētahi chatbot AI, ki hea e haere ana tēnā kōrero ki tō whakaaro? Ko wai ka pānui? E pēhea roa e tiaki ana?
2. He mea kē rānei i tukuna kē nei koe ki tētahi AI e kore e hiahia ana kia pānuia e tētahi kaiako, kaiwhakahaere, hoa ngākau rānei? (Kāore me tukuna ngā taipitopito — whakautu noa ko āe, kāo rānei.)
3. I hangaia ngā tono hoa AI kia rite ai ki ngā hoa. He aha te rereketanga i waenga i tētahi taputapu e āwhina ana ki a koe me tētahi taputapu e hiahia ana ki tōu hokia mai?
4. Mēnā ka whakamahi ana tētahi tono kore utu i ōu raraunga hei whakaako i tōna pūnaha, he tūturu tērā hokohoko? Ka huri tāu whakautu mēnā ko tētahi tamaiti 9 ngā tau tōna whakamahinga?

### Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki: Horoi hōhonu o ngā tautuhinga tūmataiti

Kōwhirihia ngā **tono e toru ia tangata e whakamahi ana rawa atu** e whai ana ki te AI. Ngā mea māmā: Snapchat, Instagram, TikTok, ChatGPT, Gemini, Character.AI, ngā ētita whakaahua, ngā kaikōrero māia.

Mō ia tono, tahi:

1. Huakina ngā tautuhinga tūmataiti.
2. Kimikimi i te wāhanga mō "raraunga i whakamahia mō te whakaako" rānei "whakapai i ō mātou pūnaha" ā, whakaaro mehemea ka whakakorehia.
3. Tirotiro he aha ka tukuna ki ngā rōpū tuatoru.
4. Arotahi ki ngā kōrero i tiakina ā, mukua ngā mea e kore e hiahia ana kia noho tonu.

Tiakina tētahi tuhinga kotahi o ā kōrua i huri. Ka tirotirohia anō ia ono marama — ka huri whakamuri ngā tautuhinga i ruia e te wā.

### Mō ngā mātua e hiahia ana ki te hōhonu ake

- [Common Sense Media — Ngā Rangatahi, Whakaaro, me te Hangarau i te Wā o ngā Hoa AI](https://www.commonsensemedia.org/research/teens-trust-and-technology-in-the-age-of-ai-companions): ngā rangahau o raro mō te whakamahinga hoa AI rangatahi.
- [Electronic Frontier Foundation — Tūmataiti Ākonga](https://www.eff.org/issues/student-privacy)

Ia tangata: ingoa tētahi mōhiotanga ōu kua whakatau koe ka kore e tūruna ki tētahi taputapu AI. I tērā wiki: me pēhea te mau i tētahi AI e kōrero hē ana ki a koe.

## TUHINGA 4 — Wiki 4: Whakaaro Māhio me te Tirotiro Kōrero

**Upoko Tautika:** He tikanga e rua miniti ka tiaki ana i a koe i ngā miihini māia, hē.

---

Ka tīpakohia tōtika ngā mea e ngā taputapu AI. Ngā take ture tīpako. Ngā tūhono rangahau tīpakohia. Ngā kōrero kāore i kōrerotia e ngā tāngata i kuhuna ki a rātou. Ko te māia, hei tohu, kua mutu tōna tikanga o nāianei.

Ehara te ārai i tētahi taputapu. He tikanga. Ko tērā e tino mohio ana au ko tērā nō te tohunga mātauranga matihiko a Mike Caulfield, ā, ko tōna ingoa ko **SIFT**.

> **S** — Tū.
> **I** — Tirotiro i te puna.
> **F** — Kimikimi i ētahi āporo pai ake.
> **T** — Whāia ngā kēmu, ngā kōrero, me ngā pāpāho ki te horopaki taketake.

Ko ērā anake. Ngā miniti e rua, ngā tāwhāiti e whā, ka piata ki ēhea mea katoa e pānuia ana, e mātakitakihia ana — i hangaia e AI, kāore rānei.

### Ko ngā āheitanga kei tō ringa ā te mutunga o tēnei wiki

- Whakamahia SIFT ki tēhea mea AI-hangaia, AI-whakapikihia rānei
- Māramatia ngā tīpako AI — ngā kōrero tīpakohia, ngā tūhono tīpako, ngā kōrero tīpakohia
- Hangaia tētahi ara tirotiro kōrero ōu ka mau ki raro i ngā miniti e rua

### Ngā pātai e whā hei mahi tahi

1. Kōrerotia e ia tangata tētahi wā i whakapono ai ia ki tētahi mea ā-ipurangi i huri ai ki te hē. He aha i whakaaro nui ai? He aha i mau ai?
2. He aha te take ka tīpako ai tētahi AI i tētahi kōrero ā, ka tukuna māia? (Tohu: pānuia anō te Wiki 1.)
3. Mēnā e kī ana tētahi TikTok, tētahi whakarāpopoto AI, me tētahi pūrongo kōrero i whakaputaina ngā mea kotahi, ko ngā puna e toru rānei — ko tētahi? Me pēhea tāu tirotiro?
4. He aha te rereketanga i waenga i *"Kāore au e mōhio ana"* me *"I tirotiroa e au ā, ehara i te tūturu"*? Ko tēhea tērā e tere ana ngā taputapu AI ki te karo?

### Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki: Māua i te AI e kōrero hē ana

Tahi, whakaritea ngā pātai e rima e *mōhio pai ana* kōrua i ō rāua:

- Te mahi a tētahi hoa whānau, i roto i ngā taipitopito
- Te tuatahi o tētahi pukapuka e pīhau ana
- Tētahi hua hākinakina ā-rohe
- Tētahi kōrero mahi iti
- Tētahi kaupeka hītori nō tō whenua

Pātaihia tētahi chatbot AI ia pātai. Whakaaro ia whakautu:

- ✅ Tika
- ⚠️ Tika ōrite (me ngā hē)
- ❌ Hē māia
- 🤷 Kāore i whakautu, i āta whakaaro rānei

Mō ia whakautu "hē māia", whakahaerehia te kēmu mā SIFT tahi. Kātahi pātaihia te pātai kino ake: *ka mau koe mēnā kāore koe i mōhio kē ki te whakautu?*

### Mō ngā mātua e hiahia ana ki te hōhonu ake

- [Te Tikanga SIFT — whakamāramatia māmā](https://guides.lib.uchicago.edu/c.php?g=1241077&p=9082322) (Whare Pukapuka o te Whare Wānanga o Chicago)
- [News Literacy Project — paepae Checkology](https://newslit.org/educators/checkology/): ngā akoranga tauwhitinga kore utu tae atu ki ngā āpure AI.
- [Pānui a Mike Caulfield — Hapgood](https://hapgood.us/): te kaihanga o SIFT, e tuhituhi māia ana.
- Tohua [Snopes](https://www.snopes.com/) me [Full Fact](https://fullfact.org/) hei whānau.

### Whakaaro kapi

Ia tangata tuhia: *"I tēnei wiki i maua e au te AI e hē ana mō ___. I maua e au tērā nā ___."* I tērā wiki, ko te akoranga tino mahi tahi o te akoranga: ko te tuhi i tō whakaaetanga AI ā-whare.

## TUHINGA 5 — Wiki 5: Te Whakarite i ngā Rohe Matihiko ā-Whare

**Upoko Tautika:** Ka hapa ngā ture me te kore tautoko. I tēnei wiki ka tuhia ngā ture tahi e kōrua.

---

Ko tēnei te akoranga tino mahi tahi i roto i te akoranga. Ehara te whāinga i tētahi pukapuka ture mātua. Ko tōna kaupeka he **whakaaetanga whare** e hainahia ana e ngā taha e rua nā te mea i āwhina ngā taha e rua ki te tuhi.

Ko te tikanga o te American Academy of Pediatrics Family Media Plan ko te papanga pai rawa atu i kitea e au. He whānui, ehara he tikanga AI — engari ka māmā ana te whakarite.

### Ko ngā āheitanga kei tō ringa ā te mutunga o tēnei wiki

- Hangaia tahi tētahi whakaaetanga AI ā-whānau e ōrite ana ki ngā mātua me ngā rangatahi
- Mātatohua te rereketanga i waenga i ngā rohe e hiahia ana ki ngā here me ngā rohe e hiahia ana ki ngā kōrero
- Māramatia ngā āhuatanga tohu whero e hiahia ana ki te uru pakiaka tūturu

### Ngā pātai e rima hei mahi tahi

1. He aha ngā whakamahinga AI e **māmā ana** ki ā kōrua? He aha ngā mea e āta whakaaro ana? He aha ngā mea i waenga?
2. Kura me ngā mahi: āhea ka rite te whakamahinga o te AI ki tētahi taputapu (pērā ki tētahi kakaroraro), āhea ka rite ki te tāwharau? Ko hea te rārangi a tō kura — ā, e whakaae ana kōrua?
3. Ngā hoa AI me te tautoko hinengaro: i raro i ēhea āhuatanga, mēnā ka pau, he hauora tōna whakamahinga? Ka huri āhea ki tētahi whakakapi mō te hononga tūturu?
4. He aha ngā hiahia o ia tangata mai i tērā atu? Rangatahi: he aha tāu e hiahia ana ko ngā mātua kia mutu? Mātua: he aha tāu e hiahia ana ko ngā rangatahi kia tīmata?
5. He aha tō ture "karanga ki tētahi tangata" — ngā āhuatanga e whakaae ana kōrua ki te tū, ki te kati i te tono, ki te kōrero ki tētahi tangata tūturu?

### Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki: Tuhia tō whakaaetanga AI ā-whare

Huakina tētahi tuhinga kotahi. Hangaia ngā wāhanga poto e rima tahi:

**1. Ā mātou whakamahi i te AI.** Āwhina mahi. Ngā kai. Whakaaro māhia. Ētita whakaahua. Kia mārama.

**2. Ā mātou kore whakamahi i te AI.** Ngā tauira hei whakaaro: tāhuri whakaahua ngā whakaahua ōu ki ngā pūnaha hanga whakaahua; tukunga ingoa ōu, tūāhuatanga noho rānei; whakakapi i tētahi hoa tūturu, kaunihera rānei.

**3. Ko ngā mea ka whakaaturia ōtia.** "Mēnā i whakamahi au i te AI mō tētahi mahi kura, ka kōrero ai au ki te kaiako mēnā e pātaihia ana." "Mēnā i ētita whakaahua AI tōku whakaahua i mua i tōku tānga, ka kī au."

**4. Ngā ture wā me wāhi.** "Kāore ngā hoa AI i muri i ngā hāora 10pm." "Ngā waea ki waho o ngā rūma moe i te pō." "Ko tētahi kai ia rā kāore he ātea."

**5. Ko te rārangi karanga ki tētahi tangata.** Ko ngā āhuatanga e mutu ai tō whakamahi i te AI ā, ka kōrero ki tētahi tangata tūturu: ngā pātai hauora hinengaro, ngā tohu mate, ngā raruraru ture, ko tēhea mea katoa e wehi ana ki a koe.

Hainahia e ngā taha e rua ā, ūruhia. Waihotia ki tētahi wāhi e kitea ana — ōrite rānei tēpū kari, papatūhono whānau. Whakaae ki tētahi rā arotahi i roto i ngā rā 90.

### Mō ngā mātua e hiahia ana ki te hōhonu ake

- [AAP Family Media Plan](https://www.healthychildren.org/English/fmp/Pages/MediaPlan.aspx): te tauira ka taea te whakarite i tūāhia tēnei mahi.
- [AAP Healthy Digital Habits Toolkit](https://www.aap.org/en/news-room/campaigns-and-toolkits/healthy-digital-habits/)
- [Common Sense Media — Aratohu Mātua Nui mō ChatGPT](https://www.commonsensemedia.org/articles/parents-ultimate-guide-to-chatgpt)
- [Center for Humane Technology](https://www.humanetech.com/): ngā mōhiotanga mō te hoahoa tango-aro.

### Whakaaro kapi

Hainahia te whakaaetanga. Tango whakaahua tahi. I tērā wiki: ko te akoranga kino rawa atu — ko te whakaata i ngā tikanga e hiahia ana koe ki tō rangatahi.

*Ngā tūtohu: ngā mātua, AI, whakaaetanga whānau, rohe matihiko, wā ātea*

## TUHINGA 6 — Wiki 6: Te Whakaata i ngā Tikanga Hangarau Pai

**Upoko Tautika:** Ka ake ake tō rangatahi i tōu mātakitakitanga i a koe i tō rāua whakarongo ki a koe. Ko tēnei wiki ka neke ngā mātua anō.

---

Ko ngā rangahau mō ngā tikanga pāpāho ā-whānau e tae ana ōtia ki te otinga kotahi: ko ngā whanonga ōna mātua e hanga ana i te hononga o ō rātou tamariki ki te hangarau i neke ake i ēhea ture. Mēnā kei runga tō waea i te tāpui kai, ka kore e tū te "kāore he waea i te tāpui kai". Mēnā ka kore e kī ana koe *"Kāore au e mōhio ana — me titiro ai,"* ka kore anō tō rangatahi.

Ko tēnei te wiki anake ka huri te aronga. Ka neke ngā mātua anō. Ka kitea.

### Ko ngā āheitanga kei tō ringa ā te mutunga o tēnei wiki

- Māramatia ko ngā rangatahi e ako ana i ngā tikanga hangarau mā te mātakitaki i ngā pakiaka o rātou
- Māramatia ngā whanonga e hiahia ana ia mema o te whānau ki te whakaata — ā, ki te mutu whakaata
- Whakaritea tētahi tikanga haere tonu māmā kia kore ai ngā akoranga o tēnei akoranga e ngaro i roto i ngā marama e toru

### Ngā pātai e rima hei mahi tahi

1. Mātua: he aha tētahi tikanga hangarau ōu ka whakamā ana koe mēnā ka tāruatia *tōtika* e tō rangatahi? Rangatahi: ko tērā pātai anō ki muri.
2. I ēhea wā ōkawa i kite ai kōrua i tērā atu ka whakahekea tētahi pūrere hei uru katoa? He pēhea tōna āhua?
3. He aha te āhua, i tēnei whare, o te whakamahi pai i te AI? Tukuna ngā tauira toru tūturu kua ora kē kōrua i roto i ngā wiki ono kua pahemo.
4. He aha tāu e hiahia ana ko te hononga o tō whānau ki te hangarau mō ngā tau e rima? Hekea whakamuri: he aha ā kōrua mahi ia tangata i tēnei marama kia tika tōna arotahi?
5. He aha ngā mea hou i a koe i roto i tēnei akoranga nō tērā atu, kāore i tūmanakohia?

### Ko te mahi o tēnei wiki: Ngā tūtohutanga Whakaata + tētahi tirotiro ā-marama

**Wāhanga A — Ngā tūtohutanga Whakaata.** Ka tuhia e ia tangata ngā **toru** whanonga e huri ana, e hanga ana hei mea ka kite ai tērā atu. Ngā tauira:

- "Ka waiho tōku waea ki te kīhini i te wā o te kai."
- "Ka kī māia e au i a au e whakamahi ana i te AI ki te tuhi i tētahi mea."
- "Ka pātai au i mua i tōku tānga i tētahi whakaahua ōu."
- "Ka kore au e whakamahi ana i tōku tono hoa AI i ngā pō kura."

Whakaahuruatia ngā rārangi. Waihotia ki te ōrite tēpū kari.

**Wāhanga B — Whakaritea te tirotiro.** Tūrua tētahi tirotiro whānau ā-marama 30 miniti ki roto i te maramataka. Ko te marama te āhua tūturu. Ko ngā pātai e toru ia wā:

1. He aha i āwhina ai te AI ki a tāua i tēnei marama?
2. He aha i utu ai te AI ki a tāua i tēnei marama?
3. Ka tika tonu tō mātou whakaaetanga whare, ka hiahia ana ki tētahi tūāhuatanga?

### Mō ngā mātua e hiahia ana ki te hōhonu ake

- [Common Sense Media — Marautanga Mana Matihiko](https://www.commonsense.org/education/digital-citizenship): marautanga hoa-roa mō ngā whānau e hiahia ana ki te haere tonu.
- [AAP — Pāpāho me ngā Tamariki](https://www.aap.org/en/patient-care/media-and-children/)
- [Center for Humane Technology — Tangohia te Mana](https://www.humanetech.com/take-control)
- [Day of AI](https://dayofai.org/): marautanga kore utu kua whakahoungia ia tau, he mea pai ki te hoki atu ia tau.

### Whakaaro kapi

Ia tangata tuhia: *"Ko te mea tino nui i akona e au i tēnei akoranga ko ___. Ko te mea tuatahi ka huri rā āpōpō ko ___."*

Ko ērā anake ko te akoranga. Ko te tuhinga whakakapi e whai ake ana.

*Ngā tūtohu: ngā mātua, whakaata, AI, ngā tikanga whānau, hangarau*

## TUHINGA 7 — Whakakapi: Ko Ngā Mea i Hangaia, me Ngā Mea kei te Heke Mai

**Upoko Tautika:** Ko ngā wiki ono ko te tīmatanga, ehara i te mutunga. Anei me pēhea te haere tonu.

---

Mēnā ko kōrua ko tō rangatahi i mahi tahi i ngā akoranga e ono katoa, tirohia ō rāua āhea nāianei:

- He kōrero ā-reo kotahi mō te kōrero mō te AI me te kore māharahara, kore noa
- He whakaaetanga AI ā-whare i hainahia me tētahi rā arotahi
- He tikanga tirotiro kōrero (SIFT) me ētahi tūmataiti-horoi ngā pūkete
- He tirotiro ā-marama i whakaritea hei pupuri i te kōrero ora
- Ko ngā huri whanonga toru ia tangata ka kite ai tērā atu mema o te whānau

He mea hanganga pai ake i tō nuinga o ngā pakiaka mō te hangarau. I hangaia tahi. Ko tērā wāhanga e nui ana.

### Ko ngā āhuatanga kāore i whai i tēnei akoranga

Ehara i te aratohu ki ngā taputapu AI katoa. Ka huri ērā. Ko te haurua o ngā mea e whakamahia ana e tō rangatahi i 2027 kāore anō i waenganui, ā, ko te haurua o ngā mea e tino nui ana āianei ka kore e nui i roto i ngā tau e rua.

### Ko ngā āhuatanga i whai

He tikanga e ū tonu ana ahakoa ko ngā mahi ā te hangarau.

- Ko te tikanga o te pātai *"he aha tonu tēnei mea e mahi ana?"* i mua i tō whakatau mehemea ka whakapono
- Ko te tikanga o te tirotiro pāpāho-ōrite e rua miniti i mua i tōu tūkino i tētahi mea
- Ko te tikanga o te kōwhiri ko ngā mea ka tukuna ki tētahi taputapu, kaua ngā mea e tango ana
- Ko te tikanga o te kī māia i a koe e whakamahi ana i te AI hei mahi i tētahi mea
- Ko te tikanga o ngā tirotiro whānau ā-marama e mau ana i ngā hūkakaoro i mua i tōna puta hei raruraru

### Ko ngā mahi e whai ake ana

- Tūrua te rā arotahi 90-rā mō tō whakaaetanga whare ki tētahi maramataka tūturu
- Tūrua te tirotiro ā-marama ki tētahi tōpenga
- Pānuia anō tō whakaaro Wiki 1 i roto i ngā marama ono ā, kite ai ko ngā hurihanga o kōrua
- Kōwhirihia tētahi o ngā hononga rauemi nō ēhea wiki rānei ā, pāpātuhia

Mēnā he whai hua tēnei akoranga ki a koe, ko te mea e tino āwhina ana ko te tukutuku i te tuhinga whakatūwhera ki tētahi atu whānau. Ka nui ngā kōrero.

Ngā mihi nui mō āu mahi tahi me tō tamaiti.

*Ngā tūtohu: AI, ngā mātua, whānau, mātauranga matihiko, whakakapi*

Rānei, tukuna katoa ngā waru hei tuhinga kore utu ā, whakamahia rātou hei tipu i te rārangi, kātahi hokona ake tētahi āhua hoahoa ake (rōpū ora, pukapuka mahi tāhurihia, āhua PDF o te marautanga) ki ngā kairapu i huri.
